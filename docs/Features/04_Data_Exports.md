# Data Exports

We believe your data belongs to you. BandMath makes it easy to export your information for accounting, marketing, or personal archives.

## Ledger Export to CSV

Need to send your financial records to an accountant for tax season? 
You can export your entire ledger to a detailed CSV file. The export includes every transaction, the exact date, the payer, the receivers, and the calculated balances.

## Email Contact List Export

If you've been using the POS system to capture customer emails at the merch table, you can easily export this list. 

The exported CSV is perfectly formatted to be imported into any major email marketing platform (like Mailchimp or Klaviyo), allowing you to seamlessly market your next tour or album release to your most loyal fans.
